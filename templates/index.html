Welcome!


To those who have never heard of an "Emulator" or a "ROM"...

This project is inspired by the online communities dedicated to storing digital copies of retro games as well as emulation tools for running them on modern PC hardware. There is a large and dedicated community online that actively searches for ROMs (read-only memory files, think a virtual CD or cartridge), and technical people who work on designing the emulators themselves. There is also a hunt for digitizing game manuals which used to come in the case that contained the CD or cartridge, which contained information about the storyline, controls, tips and tricks, and more.

This project aims to provide a means for people to easily store and view information on ROMs, emulators, publishers, and game manuals.
